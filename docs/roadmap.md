# Project Roadmap

## Phase 1: Stability
Finalizing ZMK sleep states and acoustic profiling.

## Phase 2: Input Modules
Developing an I2C-based expansion for a Pimoroni Trackball.

## Phase 3: Energy
Integrating solar film into the MCU covers for trickle-charging.

## Phase 4: Displays
Low-power E-Ink status windows for layer maps and battery life.

## Phase 5: Smart Tenting
Active stands that adjust angles to prevent static muscle loading.
